To provide an easy to use plartform for students to prepare meals based on ingredients they have at hand. It will enable students to be less dependent on campus food, and encourage self-made meals.

About Us

We are a bunch of considerate programmers looking out for our fellow stoners when they crave some immediate food. This was an idea that turned to a reality during the BCHacks hackathon. We initially had second thoughts but we decided that the community needed looking after when stomachs were empty in need of the munchies.
